The Last of Us

After a global pandemic destroys civilization, a hardened survivor takes charge of a 14-year-old girl who may be humanity’s last hope.

A series based on the critically acclaimed video game -- and starring Pedro Pascal and Bella Ramsey, among others -- is available now on HBO and HBO Max.
